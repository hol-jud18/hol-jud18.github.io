This is my first post and I just want to briefly introduce myself and what I am about. I am starting this blog to document my malware development and reverse engineering learning journey. 

I just want share what I'm building, or trying to build, and share some insights as I wander the road ahead.

## why read this

I am constantly trying to learn new things in this field and each post will have the flavor of a beginner. I am hoping that in my beginnerness, even though the language may be clumsy, that there are insights for anyone to enjoy. 

So whether you are learning this from the ground up with me or an expert in the field, I hope that my explanations can provide you something.

## what i'll write about

Posts will fall into a few categories:

- **Technique breakdowns** — implementing a specific attack technique from scratch, explaining each piece
- **RE notes** — reversing something and documenting what I find
- **Research rabbit holes** — deep-dives into Windows internals, specific APIs, or undocumented behavior
- **Project posts** — building something larger and writing about the architecture and decisions
